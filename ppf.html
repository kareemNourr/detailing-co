Paint Protection

An invisible shield between your paint and everything the road throws at it. Self-healing, hydrophobic, and warrantied for up to 12 years.

Why PPF

Your paint,
permanently protected

Paint correction and ceramic coating treat your paint after damage occurs. PPF prevents the damage from happening in the first place — an invisible armour that takes the hit so your paint doesn't have to.

Self-healing technology — minor scratches disappear with heat
Rock chip defence — absorbs impact that would otherwise chip clear coat
Hydrophobic surface — water, tar, and insects sheet off cleanly
UV protection — prevents paint fade and oxidation over years
Preserves resale value — factory paint condition commands a premium
